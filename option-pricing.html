Option Pricing Visualization

A modern tool to explore financial derivatives using Black-Scholes and Monte Carlo methods.

Project Overview

This dashboard integrates real-time market data and applies advanced pricing techniques, including the Black-Scholes model and Monte Carlo simulations. It provides users with an interactive way to visualize and understand options pricing dynamics.

Why It Matters

Understanding option pricing is essential in finance. This dashboard empowers traders, analysts, and learners to gain insights into the pricing mechanisms, evaluate market efficiencies, and enhance decision-making strategies.

Key Features

  • Real-time stock and options data via Yahoo Finance API.
  • Interactive Black-Scholes pricing and Monte Carlo simulations.
  • Intuitive visualization of stochastic price paths and option pricing comparison.
